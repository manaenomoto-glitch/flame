from the campaign: The Right to Rest Brina is a 14-year-old apprentice mage from Emberhill. She's part of the Arcane Engineers subgroup of F.L.A.M.E. She says: I was expected to stay up three nights in a row enchanting scrolls for a royal gala. No one asked if I needed sleep, food, or time to recharge. I made a mistake on the final scroll and it exploded in my face. The senior wizard blamed me. This isn’t just my story. Lots of apprentices are burning out—literally. This is why I joined the Right to Rest campaign. It's not lazy to rest—it's responsible. Main issues Brina faced: No sleep schedule No lunch breaks No protection for magical burnout The F.L.A.M.E. union is helping me speak up. I finally feel heard.
