F . L . A . M . E

Federation of Laborers, Artisans, Mages & Engineers

"Keep the H.L.A.M.B. of latrness burning!"

🔥 Campaign: The Right to Rest (and Respawn)

We are tired of being tired. Mages, smiths, engineers, and potion brewers are working long hours without proper breaks. We're not asking for magic — just a moment to eat our stew in peace

Our 3 Main Campaign Goals

  1. The 4-Hour Rule - Every worker gets a break every 4 hours. Even during dragon sieges.
  2. Universal Bedroll Access - Free, flameproof bedrolls for all guild-certified workers.
  3. Respawn Leave - If you're resurrected due to unsafe working conditions, you get the day off. No arguments.

Slogans You'll See Around Town

  • "Breaks before Blades!"
  • "No Stew, No Spells!"
  • "Stop the Grind — Nap Time Now!"
